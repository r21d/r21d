Achieve Lossless Audio

1. Create a new project at 192kHz rate. Record with the highest available settings to achieve a near-to-real audio signal.

2. These high-fidelity stems can either be used in a new project or you can change the project settings to 96kHz or even 48kHz. You are not losing fidelity by adjusting sample rates after recording.

3. Render your mix in 192kHz, 32-bit. If the mix sounds too reverby or has other issues, adjust the mix, not the sample rate.

4. Open a fresh project at 192kHz, import the stereo mix, and add a mastering chain. Render the final master at 192kHz, 32-bit.

5. (Optional) Remaster the high-fidelity master to a lower bitrate and depth as per the delivery method specs. This may involve dithering or using Adobe Media Encoder.
